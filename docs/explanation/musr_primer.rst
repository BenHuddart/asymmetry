Muon-spin spectroscopy: a primer
================================

.. note::

   This primer is being written. It is not a substitute for the primary
   literature — see :doc:`comparison` and the references cited on the
   individual :doc:`/reference/fit_functions/index` pages.

This page gives a short, self-contained introduction to muon-spin spectroscopy
(μSR): how implanted spin-polarised muons act as a local probe of magnetic
fields, what the measured asymmetry represents, and how the polarisation
functions used throughout the reference pages arise. It assumes undergraduate
physics but no prior knowledge of the technique.
